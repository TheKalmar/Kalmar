# QR Payment Flow

## Goal
Customer scans QR and completes payment with minimal friction.

## Actor
Customer

## Steps
1. Scan QR
2. Open payment page
3. Review merchant and amount
4. Select payment method
5. Confirm payment
6. Show success or failure result

## Edge cases
- Invalid QR
- Session expired
- Payment failed
- User closes page

## Notes for UI/UX
Keep trust elements visible: merchant name, amount, status clarity.
